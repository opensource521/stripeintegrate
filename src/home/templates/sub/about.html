About Us




 

Who Are We

We are members of Balance Tech Private Limited - a private company incorporated in Singapore in 2016. Our team represents a group of individuals who are passionate about using technology to improve the education and literacy in singapore.


Our Mission

With private education on the rise, we aspire to build more simplicity and transparency into the process of private tutoring. With the rapid rise of technology, we are witnessing tutors who have limited knowledge of digital marketing being left behind, but that does not need to be so.


How We Can Help

We have built https://www.teach-advisor.com/, a digital platform to support and empower tutors to manage their own tuition jobs, market their services, monitor their progress and build their own brand by accumulating positive reviews.
